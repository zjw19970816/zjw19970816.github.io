About me

My name is Jiatong SONG. I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Civil Engineering, The University of Hong Kong (HKU) starting in Sep. 2020, supervised by Prof. W.Y. Szeto (HKU). I am a member of HKU ITS.

I received my BEng degree from the Department of Traffic and Transportation Engineering, Central South University (CSU) in July, 2019.

My research mainly focuses on the area of Network modeling and bike-sharing, especially bike-sharing system design and shared e-bike system.
