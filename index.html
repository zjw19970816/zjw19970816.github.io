About me

My name is Wentao Ning. I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Computer Science, The University of Hong Kong (HKU) from Sep. 2020 under HKU-SUSTech Joint PhD Programme, supervised by Prof. Reynold Cheng (HKU) and Prof. Bo Tang (SUSTech). I am a member of HKU STAR Lab and DBGroup@SUSTech.

I received my BEng degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in July, 2020.

My research mainly focuses on the area of data mining and machine learning, especially recommender systems and graph neural network (GNN).
